Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about custom songs, delivery, revisions, and more.

How long does it take?
Melody Starter ~5 days; Harmony Deluxe ~3 days priority; Symphony Ultimate can include a 72-hour rush (subject to availability).
What files do I receive?
Starter: high-quality MP3. Deluxe adds vocal + instrumental stems and a designed lyric PDF. Ultimate includes a custom video montage and premium printed lyrics.
Can I request changes?
Deluxe includes one revision; Ultimate includes unlimited revisions during the active project window. Starters can upgrade revision options anytime.
How do payments work?
After you submit your order details, we’ll email next steps with payment options and timelines.
Do you do rush orders?
Yes—subject to schedule. Ultimate includes 72-hour rush where available. Reach out on the contact page.
